Together, we can create a school system where every teacher INSPIRES and every student THRIVES.

At Equity Consulting Group, we are dedicated to revolutionizing education through empowering teachers, leaders, and administrators with the mindset and skills needed to create profound learning environments. With over 40 collective years of experience, we envision a world where every learner has access to equitable, high-quality education that embraces cultural responsiveness and unlocks their full potential. Our collaborative approach is grounded in inclusion, social justice, and innovative strategies that access students' funds of knowledge, fostering academic excellence while cultivating an intrinsic love of learning. Together, we're driving systemic change that creates sustainable transformation for present and future generations.

Mission

Our mission at Equity Consulting Group is to transform educational systems through equity-centered approaches that empower teachers, leaders, and students. We are dedicated to creating environments where every educator can reach their full potential and every student has access to high-quality learning opportunities. Through collaborative partnerships, innovative solutions, and a deep commitment to educational equity, we strive to dismantle barriers and build capacity for excellence in education across diverse communities.

Equity Consulting Group has been a valued partner for our school organization. Equity originally helped us to design our curriculum with a very culturally relevant lens.
